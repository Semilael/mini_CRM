Superuser:

Full access rights to all platform functions.
User and role management.
Ability to add, edit, and delete any data.
Administrator:

User and role management.
Access to administrative settings of the platform.
Access to question and answer management, including adding and deleting.
Content Editor:

Ability to add and edit content, including images.
Access to question and answer management.
Limited access rights to administrative settings.
Moderator:

Review and approval of questions and answers.
Content and image management.
Limited access rights to administrative settings.
User:

Ability to ask questions and receive answers.
Addition and viewing of content with images.
Limited access rights to administrative settings.
Guest:

View content, questions, and answers without creating an account.
Limited access to functions requiring authentication.
